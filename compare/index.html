Which app should you choose?

Start with the job you need done.

Choose forScore if...

You mainly need a professional iPad music stand for yourself: reading, annotating, setlists, page turns and performance.

Choose MobileSheets if...

You mainly need to organise a large personal PDF library, especially if Android is your main device.

Choose Newzik if...

You work with ensembles, orchestras or groups where many musicians need to collaborate on the same repertoire.

Choose Melopal if...

You teach one-to-one lessons and want every student to leave with the right music, instructions and recordings in one place.
