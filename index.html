Fair questions

You’re skeptical. Good - you’re a teacher.

Why not just keep using WhatsApp, email and Drive?

Because it's cluttered and messy. A chat scrolls away, a folder grows crowded, and nothing connects them. Melopal keeps the piece, the instructions and the references together for each student. That is the missing connection.

Will my students really open another app?

They open the app where their music lives - that’s a different thing than “another app.” Joining is a QR scan in your lesson, no email needed for young students, and afterwards it’s the only place they have to remember.

Is this more admin for me?

An assignment takes about 30 seconds - pick student, two lines, attach. That usually replaces an email, a chat message, and the “what was it again?” follow-ups. You send it once. Your student always knows where to find it.
