The fine print, in plain words

Questions about pricing, answered.

Do my students or their parents pay?

No. The teacher is the customer, and students join for free through your invite. We want every student to be able to practice without cost getting in the way.

Do I need a credit card to try it?

No. Sign up with your name and email, and you’re in. The Free plan stays free forever, so you can use Melopal for real with your real students right away.

What happens to my library if I stop using Melopal?

Your PDFs are your PDFs. The material you upload stays yours to take with you. Holding your sheet music hostage isn't a business model we're interested in.
