Melopal Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Melopal is a teacher-student workspace for music education. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

When using Melopal, we may collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Account information
  • Student and teacher profile information
  • Sheet music and PDF files uploaded by users
  • Assignment instructions, notes, messages, and comments
  • Audio recordings uploaded by users
  • Technical information required to operate the service, such as device, browser, and diagnostic information

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide the Melopal service
  • Store and synchronize assignments and uploaded materials
  • Enable communication between teachers and students
  • Improve reliability, security, and performance
  • Respond to support requests

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Sharing of Information

Information is shared only as necessary to operate the service.

Teachers can access information belonging to students connected to their account. Students can access information shared with them by their teachers.

We may use trusted third-party service providers for hosting, storage, authentication, analytics, payment processing, and technical operations.

Data Retention

Data is retained while an account remains active and for a reasonable period afterward as required to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account through the app or by contacting support.

Deleting an account may remove access to assignments, uploaded files, notes, recordings, and other account-related content.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information. However, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

Student's Data

Melopal is designed for educational use. Teachers are responsible for ensuring that their use of Melopal complies with applicable laws and requirements regarding students.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Melopal after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
