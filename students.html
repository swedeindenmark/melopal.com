For students & parents

Everything your teacher wants you to practice. One place.

No more scrolling through chats for that one PDF. Your assigned music, your teacher’s notes and the recordings that show how it should sound - together, on whatever device you are using. Melopal is free for students.

What you see

Open the app. Know exactly what to do.

  • This week’s music - the actual sheet music, not a “check your email.”
  • Your teacher’s words - written notes or a voice message, attached right to the piece.
  • How it should sound - YouTube or Spotify references, one tap away.
  • Your progress - see what’s new, in progress, or completed.

Joining Melopal

You’ll be set up before the lesson ends.

Your teacher sends the invite - you don’t install anything blind or fill out forms.

Step 1

Scan the QR code

Your teacher shows it in the lesson, or sends you a link. That’s the whole invitation.

Step 2

Say who you are

Young students don’t even need an email address - a name is enough to get started.

Step 3

Your music is waiting

Anything your teacher already assigned is right there. From now on, this is where you practice.

For parents

Know what “practice your violin” actually means this week.

You want to help, but you’re not at the lesson. Melopal makes the teacher’s plan visible: which piece, which bars, what to listen to. Helping your child practice stops being detective work.

  • See the current assignment at a glance - no digging through chats
  • Read the teacher's instructions, and support your child's practice
  • Young students can join without an email address - just a first name to get started.
